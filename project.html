Lara Ferro is an art director and producer with more than 7 years of experience.

She lives and works between
São Paulo ⟶ London

For years, she dedicated herself to being a project manager at Kolor, a production company founded by the Franco-Hungarian photographer Pol Kurucz, where she worked on concept development as a creative.

She has also worked as a coordinator, creative / executive producer and service provider in fashion, art and advertising. She has ample experience in audiovisual and cultural projects, advertising campaigns, photography and digital art.

After living in London, she is currently pursuing a Masters Degree in Multimedia Art at the University of Fine Arts in Lisbon.
